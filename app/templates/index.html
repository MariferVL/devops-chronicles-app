DevOps Chronicles

Embark on your epic quest!

In this realm, you will forge legendary DevOps heroes and venture into thrilling quests that challenge the might of automation and bring order to digital chaos.

API Endpoints

  • POST /heroes/ - Create a new DevOps hero
  • GET /heroes/ - Retrieve all heroes
  • GET /heroes/<hero_id> - Retrieve hero details
  • PUT /heroes/<hero_id> - Update hero attributes
  • DELETE /heroes/<hero_id> - Delete a DevOps hero
  • POST /adventures/ - Initiate a new adventure challenge
  • GET /adventures/<adventure_id> - View adventure details
  • GET /adventures/history - See the log of all adventures
